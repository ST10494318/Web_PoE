About This Event

Join us for a comprehensive community clean-up in Brackenfell! We'll be focusing on cleaning up public parks, streets, and common areas to make our community cleaner and more beautiful for everyone. This event is perfect for individuals, families, and groups looking to make a tangible difference in their local environment.

Event Schedule

08:45 - 09:00 - Registration & Welcome

09:00 - 09:15 - Safety Briefing & Team Allocation

09:15 - 11:30 - Clean-up Activities

11:30 - 12:00 - Waste Collection & Refreshments

12:00 - Group Photo & Closing

Areas We'll Cover

  • Brackenfell Public Park and playground areas
  • Surrounding street areas and sidewalks
  • School perimeter zones and sports fields
  • Public walking paths and green spaces
  • Community garden areas

What We'll Achieve Together

  • Remove litter and waste from public spaces
  • Promote community pride and environmental awareness
  • Properly sort recyclable materials for processing
  • Create a cleaner, safer environment for all residents
  • Inspire ongoing community involvement in environmental protection
  • Collect data on waste types for future prevention strategies
