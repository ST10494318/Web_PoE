Quick answers to common enquiries

Our school workshops start from R500 for a 1-hour session for up to 30 students. Costs may vary based on duration, materials required, and travel distance. We offer discounted rates for multiple sessions and under-resourced schools.

We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance for school workshops and 1-2 weeks for community clean-ups. For larger events or corporate partnerships, we suggest contacting us 4-6 weeks ahead.
