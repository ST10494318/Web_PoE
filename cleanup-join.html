Safety Guidelines

Important information for a safe and successful clean-up

  • Wear appropriate clothing and closed shoes
  • Bring any necessary medications
  • Eat a good breakfast and stay hydrated
  • Check the weather forecast and dress accordingly
  • Arrive 15 minutes early for registration and briefing
  • Always wear provided gloves and safety gear
  • Work in pairs or small groups
  • Stay within designated clean-up areas
  • Don't touch sharp objects or hazardous materials
  • Take regular breaks and drink water
  • Follow instructions from team leaders
  • Don't pick up syringes, needles, or broken glass
  • Don't enter private property or restricted areas
  • Don't lift heavy objects alone
  • Don't approach wildlife or disturb habitats
  • Don't clean up hazardous chemicals or unknown substances
  • Notify a team leader immediately
  • First aid kit and trained personnel are available
  • Emergency contact numbers are provided at registration
  • Know the location of the nearest medical facility
  • Follow emergency evacuation procedures if needed
